About Us

A new name in Nagpur's plotting business, built on an old promise

Shree Developers is founded on a simple idea: land ownership should be transparent, secure and within reach. Every plot we release is RL NMRDA approved, carries a clear legal title, and sits within a layout planned for developed roads, water and electricity connections, cemented internal roads, and open green space.

We're a new company — and we'd rather earn your trust with paperwork and site visits than promises. Come walk the layout, verify the approvals, and decide for yourself.
